Seasonal Beers

Named for the Adirondack Mountains in Victoria’s home state of New York, our autumn seasonal is made with maple from Victoria’s college and we added a little cinnamon to finish off the fall flavors. Perfect for enjoying by the bonfire or campfire this autumn.
Coddiwomple (v.) | Origin: English Slang Word. | Definition: To travel in a purposeful manner towards a vague destination. This is our spring seasonal. The flavors of fresh strawberry and kiwi make this the perfect beer for any of your spring adventures!
Strikhedonia (n.) | Origin: Greek | Definition: The pleasure of being able to say “to hell with it!” With the addition of guava and passion fruit, this hefeweizen takes an even more summer-y twist. The perfect companion for all of your exciting adventures this summer!
